ABOUT US

AB Tech One Solution is a newly established tech sales company founded by two proud Taiping boys with a passion for technology and innovation. Our journey begins with a simple yet ambitious mission: to provide individuals and businesses with reliable access to top-quality tech products and solutions.

We offer a diverse range of products, including IT devices such as computers, laptops, tablets, smartphones, storage solutions, and accessories, sourced from trusted global brands.

Our expertise also extends to security systems with CCTV setups and business solutions like point-of-sale and accounting systems from leading providers.

Though we’re just starting, our vision is clear—to empower our community and clients with the tools and technology needed to succeed in an increasingly digital world.

AB Tech One Solution is committed to building a reputation for exceptional service, trustworthy advice, and the latest tech innovations.
